Persona

End User - The audience is a producer or creative art director of a company looking to hire a storyboard artist. This person has experience managing people and has been in the art industry for a while. They think they've seen everything so they really need something innovative to grab their attention and even devote time to look at an applicant's portfolio. They have been in the field for a least a few years so they know what they want and what they don't want.
